News

Latest updates from Yale School of Art

Spring Exhibition Opens

A new collection of student and faculty work on display.

The exhibition features over 50 works created by both undergraduate and graduate students. Visitors can explore a wide range of mediums including painting, sculpture, photography, and digital installations.


The gallery will remain open through May 15 and is free to the public during regular hours.

Student Showcase 2026

Featuring emerging artists across multiple disciplines.

The Student Showcase highlights innovative work from across all departments. This year’s exhibition includes painting, graphic design, sculpture, and printmaking.


Students gain exposure to faculty, visiting critics, and the public, making this one of the most important events of the academic year.

Guest Artist Lecture

Join visiting artists for an interactive session.

This lecture series brings internationally recognized artists to campus to share their experiences and creative processes.


Students will have the opportunity to engage directly with the artist during a Q&A session following the presentation.
