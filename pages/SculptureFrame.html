Sculpture (MFA)

Explore form, material, and spatial practice in a dynamic studio environment for experimental sculpture.

Sculpture Studio

Program Overview

The Sculpture MFA emphasizes critical making, material exploration, and conceptual development. Students work across processes to create spatial, object-based, and installation-driven work in a collaborative studio culture.

🔹
Material experimentation Work with casting, carving, fabrication.
➕
Studio critique & installation Develop ambitious work with faculty support.

Program Details

Length:2 years
Areas of Focus:3D form, installation, casting, fabrication.
Facilities:Large, well-equipped studios, sculpture shop, galleries.
Cross-disciplinary opportunities: Experiment with material, scale, and spatial storytelling. Woodshop, metal shop, studio labs, critique spaces.
