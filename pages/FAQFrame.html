Admissions FAQ

Find answers to common questions about applying, submitting portfolios, deadlines, and preparing for Yale School of Art MFA admission.

Popular Questions
What are the portfolio requirements? +
Applicants should submit 15–20 recent works in approved digital formats. Include concise descriptions when helpful for context.
When is the application deadline? +
Priority review is January 9, 2025. Final deadlines vary by program.
Do I need a statement of purpose? +
Yes. Applicants should explain their practice, goals, and interest in the program.
How can I ask admissions a question? +
Use the contact form, attend an event, or send a message to admissions.
Is there an application fee? +
Yes, the application fee is $105. Fee waivers are available — contact admissions for details.
